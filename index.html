PURPLE ACADEMY

At Purple we are committed to the training and development of our staff and offer a bespoke training program designed to nurture key skills and develop the depth of knowledge required to be a top billing consultant.

The Purple Training Academy is an exciting new venture for Purple which takes new recruits through a dedicated industry leading, step by step learning process and ‘on the job’ training, providing you with all of the tools a recruit would require. Along with all this, there is the possibility of being promoted to fully fledged consultants within 6 months in this target driven, fast paced sales environment, with on-going bespoke training sessions, mentoring and extensive personal development.

Get in contact if you have:
  • Proven B2B sales background and previous experience of meeting and exceeding targets
  • Experience of working in a fast paced, pressured, telephone sales role
  • A strong interest in building relationships
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Strong interpersonal skills
  • Self-motivation

If you want to join the Purple Academy, contact Matthew Williams

PURPLE LIFE

Purple have a wonderful culture and an expert balance of work and play. With quarterly socials, company holidays and award ceremonies there is often a lot to celebrate.

When a hard days work is complete, the team can be found out and about together whether it be having a kick about with the Purple football team or joining the Purple gym bunnies for a workout at Virgin Active.

When we want to unwind the Purple team can be found sharing a pint at one of the hundreds of local watering holes, serenading each other at karaoke or munching in top notch eateries.
